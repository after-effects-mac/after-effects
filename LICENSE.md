## FAQ ❓

### Is After Effects free for Mac?

No, but a 7-day free trial is available with full features. Creative Cloud subscriptions start at $20.99/month for individuals. Students get 60%+ discount.

### Do I need an internet connection?

Yes for initial installation and monthly license verification. After Effects can work offline for up to 99 days.

### Does it work on MacBook Air?

Yes, especially M1 and newer models. Complex 3D scenes may be slower, but 2D motion graphics run smoothly.

### Is After Effects better on Mac or Windows?

Both platforms perform well, but Mac offers better Apple Silicon optimization and smoother integration with Final Cut Pro and Logic Pro.

### Can I use After Effects with Premiere Pro?

Yes! Dynamic Link allows seamless round-tripping between apps. Changes in After Effects update instantly in Premiere.

### What's the best Mac for After Effects?

Mac Studio with M2 Ultra and 64GB+ RAM for professionals. M3 MacBook Pro with 36GB RAM for portability.

### How do I learn After Effects?

Adobe offers free tutorials. YouTube channels like Video Copilot, Jake In Motion, and SonduckFilm have excellent beginner guides.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues 🔧

- **Won't Launch**: Update macOS, reinstall Creative Cloud, check permissions
- **Slow Preview**: Reduce resolution, enable Fast Draft, clear cache
- **Crash on Export**: Disable hardware acceleration, update GPU drivers (Intel Macs)
- **Plugins Not Showing**: Install in correct folder (/Applications/Adobe After Effects/Plug-ins/)
